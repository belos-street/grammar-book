## 情景对话练习

### 对话一：约时间

**A: Hi Tom, are you free tomorrow?**
**B: Let me check my schedule. Yes, I'm free. What's up?**
**A: I want to meet you at 3 o'clock. Is that okay?**
**B: 3 o'clock? Sure! Where shall we meet?**
**A: Let's meet at the coffee shop on Main Street.**
**B: Great! See you tomorrow at 3.**
**A: See you then!**

> **发音要点**：
> - tomorrow /təˈmɒrəʊ/：重音在第二个音节
> - schedule /ˈʃedjuːl/：重音在第一个音节
> - coffee /ˈkɒfi/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话二：问价格

**A: Excuse me, how much is this jacket?**
**B: It's 150 dollars.**
**A: Oh, that's expensive. Do you have anything cheaper?**
**B: Yes, we have this one on sale. It's only 80 dollars.**
**A: Hmm, that's better. What about these shoes?**
**B: They're 60 dollars.**
**A: Okay, I'll take the shoes.**

> **发音要点**：
> - jacket /ˈdʒækɪt/：重音在第一个音节
> - expensive /ɪkˈspensɪv/：重音在第二个音节
> - cheaper /ˈtʃiːpə/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话三：说生日

**A: When is your birthday, Mary?**
**B: My birthday is on June 15th.**
**A: June 15th? That's next month! What year were you born?**
**B: I was born in 2005. How about you?**
**A: My birthday is October 1st. I'm older than you.**
**B: Really? How old are you now?**
**A: I'm 20 years old.**
**B: I'm 19. You're only one year older!**

> **发音要点**：
> - birthday /ˈbɜːθdeɪ/：重音在第一个音节
> - born /bɔːn/：单音节
> - October /ɒkˈtəʊbə/：重音在第二个音节

## 语法重点

### 1. 时间表达法

**o'clock（整点）**
- It's five o'clock. (5点整)

**past（过）**
- It's ten past two. (2点10分)

**half past（半点）**
- It's half past seven. (7点半)

**quarter（四分之一）**
- It's a quarter past three. (3点15分)
- It's a quarter to nine. (8点45分)

**to（差）**
- It's twenty to four. (3点40分)

### 2. 日期顺序

**月份 + 日期 + 年份**
- March 15th, 2024
- July 1st, 2023
- December 25th, 2022

### 3. 价格单位

**dollars (美元)**
- 1 dollar = 100 cents

**yuan (人民币)**
- 1 yuan = 10 jiao = 100 fen

**pounds (英镑)**
- 1 pound = 100 pence

## 常见错误

### 1. 数字发音混淆
- **错误**：three /triː/ vs tree
- **正确**：three /θriː/，注意/θ/的发音
- **原因**：/θ/是清辅音，需要咬舌

### 2. 十几和几十混淆
- **错误**：fifteen /ˈfɪftiːn/ 读成 /ˈfɪfti/
- **正确**：fifteen有尾音/-tiːn/，fifty没有
- **原因**：-teen表示"十几"，-ty表示"几十"

### 3. 序数词拼写错误
- **错误**：fifth写成fiveth，twelve写成twelvth
- **正确**：fifth, twelfth
- **原因**：序数词有特殊变化

### 4. 日期顺序错误
- **错误**：15th March
- **正确**：March 15th (美式) / 15th March (英式)
- **原因**：美式先说月，英式先说日

### 5. 时间表达混淆
- **错误**：It's ten to one. 理解成10点到1点
- **正确**：It's ten to one. = 12:50
- **原因**：to表示"差"，差10分到1点

## 学习建议

1. **背诵基础数字**：1-100的数字要熟练，这是基础中的基础
2. **区分-teen和-ty**：fifteen vs fifty，thirteen vs thirty
3. **练习序数词**：特别是first, second, third等不规则变化
4. **掌握时间表达**：past和to的用法是重点
5. **多听多读日期**：特别是生日、节日等实际场景
6. **练习价格问答**：在购物时主动用英语问价
