# Level 1 Chapter 6: 感谢、道歉、礼貌回应

## 章节目标

- **核心目标**：掌握最基本礼貌口语
- **语法**：简短答句、礼貌省略句
- **句型**：Thank you. You're welcome. / Sorry. That's OK. / Excuse me.
- **场景**：所有日常礼貌场景

---

## 知识点概览

### 一、感谢用语

**基本感谢**
- Thank you. / Thanks. (谢谢你)
- Thank you very much. (非常感谢你)
- Thank you so much. (太感谢你了)
- Thanks a lot. (多谢)

**感谢特定帮助**
- Thank you for your help. (谢谢你的帮助)
- Thank you for waiting. (谢谢你的等待)
- Thanks for the information. (谢谢你的信息)

**如何回应感谢**
- You're welcome. (不客气)
- No problem. (没问题)
- No worries. (别担心)
- It's my pleasure. / My pleasure. (是我的荣幸)
- Don't mention it. (不用客气)
- That's OK. / That's all right. (没关系)

### 二、道歉用语

**基本道歉**
- Sorry. / I'm sorry. (对不起)
- I apologize. (我道歉)
- I'm so sorry. (非常抱歉)
- I'm very sorry. (我很抱歉)
- Sorry about that. (为此抱歉)

**为何道歉**
- Sorry for being late. (抱歉迟到了)
- Sorry for the wait. (抱歉让您久等)
- Sorry for bothering you. (抱歉打扰你)
- Sorry, my mistake. (抱歉，我的错)

**如何回应道歉**
- That's OK. (没关系)
- It's OK. (没事)
- No problem. (没问题)
- Don't worry about it. (别担心)
- It's all right. (没关系)
- It's fine. (没关系)

### 三、Excuse Me 用法

**引起注意**
- Excuse me, can you help me? (打扰一下，你能帮我吗？)
- Excuse me, where is the bathroom? (打扰一下，洗手间在哪儿？)

**请求通过**
- Excuse me, can I get through? (打扰一下，我能过去吗？)
- Excuse me, coming through. (借过。)

**表示歉意（轻微打扰）**
- Excuse me, I sneezed. (抱歉，我打了个喷嚏)
- Excuse me, that was me. (抱歉，那是我

**失礼后道歉**
- Excuse me, I didn't mean to push. (抱歉，我不是故意推的)
- Pardon me. (请原谅我)

### 四、礼貌回应总结

| 场景 | 回应方式 |
|------|----------|
| 别人说 Thank you | You're welcome. / No problem. / My pleasure. |
| 别人说 Sorry | That's OK. / No problem. / Don't worry. |
| 需要打扰别人 | Excuse me. |
| 别人说 You're welcome | 微笑点头即可 |

## 情景对话练习

### 对话一：接受帮助后

**A: Thank you so much for helping me.**
**B: You're welcome! Happy to help.**
**A: I really appreciate it.**
**B: No problem at all. That's what friends are for.**

> **发音要点**：
> - appreciate /əˈpriːʃieɪt/：重音在第二个音节
> - friends /frendz/：/e/发音

### 对话二：餐厅用餐

**A: Excuse me, can I order?**
**B: Of course, what would you like?**
**A: I'll have the steak, please.**
**B: Sure! Would you like something to drink?**
**A: Yes, a glass of water, please.**
**B: Here you are.**
**A: Thank you!**
**B: You're welcome. Enjoy your meal!**

> **发音要点**：
> - order /ˈɔːdə/：重音在第一个音节
> - steak /steɪk/：/eɪ/发音
> - enjoy /ɪnˈdʒɔɪ/：重音在第二个音节

### 对话三：迟到道歉

**A: I'm so sorry I'm late.**
**B: That's OK, don't worry about it.**
**A: The traffic was really bad.**
**B: It's all right. These things happen.**
**A: Thanks for waiting for me.**
**B: No problem! Let's go.**

> **发音要点**：
> - traffic /ˈtræfɪk/：重音在第一个音节
> - happen /ˈhæpən/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话四：办公室场景

**A: Excuse me, do you have a moment?**
**B: Sure, what do you need?**
**A: I'm sorry to bother you, but I have a question about the report.**
**B: No problem at all. Ask away.**
**A: Thank you. I really appreciate your help.**
**B: You're welcome! Happy to help.**

> **发音要点**：
> - report /rɪˈpɔːt/：重音在第二个音节
> - question /ˈkwestʃən/：重音在第一个音节

## 语法重点

### 1. Thank you 的回答

**正式场合**
- You're welcome.
- My pleasure.
- It's my pleasure.

**非正式场合**
- No problem.
- No worries.
- That's OK.

**更随意**
- Sure!
- No big deal.

### 2. Sorry 的回答

**最常用**
- That's OK.
- It's OK.
- No problem.

**安慰对方**
- Don't worry about it.
- It's all right.
- It's fine.

### 3. Excuse Me vs Sorry

**Excuse me = 打扰一下（事前）**
- 用于引起注意
- 用于请求通过
- 用于轻微打扰

**Sorry = 对不起（事后）**
- 用于道歉
- 用于认错
- 用于表示遗憾

## 常见错误

### 1. Thank you 回答用 not at all
- **错误**：Not at all. (这不是Thank you的回答)
- **正确**：You're welcome. / No problem.
- **原因**：not at all在英语中更多用于回应"谢谢"或"抱歉"

### 2. Sorry和Excuse me混用
- **错误**：Sorry, where's the bathroom? (应该用Excuse me)
- **正确**：Excuse me, where's the bathroom?
- **原因**：问路是事前打扰，用Excuse me

### 3. Thank you too 错误
- **错误**：Thank you too much.
- **正确**：Thank you very much.
- **原因**：too much不与thank you连用

### 4. You're welcome. 过度使用
- **错误**：每次说Thank you都回You're welcome
- **正确**：可以用No problem, Sure, My pleasure等
- **原因**：多样化的回应更自然

### 5. Sorry的回答用You're welcome
- **错误**：别人道歉后说You're welcome
- **正确**：That's OK. / No problem.
- **原因**：You're welcome是感谢的回应，不是道歉的回应

## 学习建议

1. **熟练使用基本礼貌用语**：Thank you, Sorry, Excuse me是每天都会用到的
2. **掌握多种回应方式**：不要只会一种，要多样化表达
3. **注意使用场合**：Excuse me用于事前，Sorry用于事后
4. **在真实场景中练习**：主动说谢谢，主动道歉
5. **注意语气和表情**：礼貌不仅是语言，还有态度
6. **不要过度道歉**：西方文化中过度道歉会显得不自然
