# Level 1 Chapter 5: 问路 & 简单需求表达

## 章节目标

- **核心目标**：提出简单请求、找地方
- **语法**：情态动词 can / 祈使句
- **句型**：Where is…? / Can you help me? / I want / I need…
- **场景**：问路、找厕所、找车站、求助

---

## 知识点概览

### 一、情态动词 can

**can的基本用法**

| 肯定句 | 否定句 | 疑问句 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| I can swim. | I cannot (can't) swim. | Can you swim? |
| You can go. | You cannot (can't) go. | Can I go? |
| He can help. | He cannot (can't) help. | Can he help? |

**can的意思**
- 表示能力（会、能）
- 表示允许（可以）
- 表示请求（能否）

### 二、祈使句

**肯定祈使句**
- Sit down, please. (请坐下)
- Open the door. (开门)
- Wait here. (在这等)

**否定祈使句**
- Don't sit here. (别坐这儿)
- Don't worry. (别担心)
- Don't be late. (别迟到)

### 三、问路常用句型

**问地点**
- Where is the bathroom? (洗手间在哪儿？)
- Where is the train station? (火车站在哪儿？)
- Where is the nearest hotel? (最近的酒店在哪儿？)

**问方向**
- How do I get to...? (我怎么去...？)
- Which way is...? (哪条路是...？)
- Is it far from here? (离这儿远吗？)

### 四、需求表达

**I want... (我想要...)**
- I want a cup of coffee. (我想要一杯咖啡)
- I want to go home. (我想回家)
- I want some help. (我想要一些帮助)

**I need... (我需要...)**
- I need help. (我需要帮助)
- I need to use the bathroom. (我需要用洗手间)
- I need a taxi. (我需要一辆出租车)

**Can I have...? (我能要...吗？)**
- Can I have some water? (我能要些水吗？)
- Can I have the menu? (我能看下菜单吗？)
- Can I have your name? (我能知道你的名字吗？)

- I cannot (can't) swim. (我不会游泳)

**2. 请求否定**
- I can't understand. (我不懂)
- I can't find it. (我找不到)

## 情景对话练习

### 对话一：问路

**A: Excuse me, can you help me?**
**B: Sure, what do you need?**
**A: Where is the train station?**
**B: Go straight for two blocks, then turn left. It's on your right.**
**A: Is it far from here?**
**B: It's about a ten-minute walk.**
**A: Thank you so much!**
**B: You're welcome!**

> **发音要点**：
> - excuse /ɪkˈskjuːz/：重音在第二个音节
> - straight /streɪt/：/eɪ/发音
> - minute /ˈmɪnɪt/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话二：找厕所

**A: Sorry, can you help me?**
**B: Of course!**
**A: Where is the bathroom?**
**B: It's down the hall on the right.**
**A: Thank you!**
**B: No problem!**

> **发音要点**：
> - bathroom /ˈbɑːθruːm/：重音在第一个音节
> - down /daʊn/：双元音/aʊ/
> - hall /hɔːl/：长元音/ɔː/

### 对话三：求助

**A: Can you help me? I need some help.**
**B: Sure, what happened?**
**A: I lost my passport. I don't know what to do.**
**B: Don't worry. Did you check your hotel?**
**A: Yes, I called them. It's not there.**
**B: Maybe you should go to the police station.**
**A: Where is the police station?**
**B: It's near the city hall.**
**A: Thank you for your help!**
**B: You're welcome. Good luck!**

> **发音要点**：
> - passport /ˈpɑːspɔːt/：重音在第一个音节
> - police /pəˈliːs/：重音在第二个音节
> - station /ˈsteɪʃən/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话四：点餐

**A: Can I help you?**
**B: Yes, I want a hamburger and a Coke.**
**A: Sure. Would you like some fries?**
**B: Yes, please. And I need a glass of water.**
**A: Anything else?**
**B: No, that's all. Thank you.**

> **发音要点**：
> - hamburger /ˈhæmbɜːɡə/：重音在第一个音节
> - would /wʊd/：注意/w/音
> - fries /fraɪz/：/aɪ/发音

## 语法重点

### 1. can的用法

**表示能力**
- I can speak three languages. (我会说三种语言)
- She can play the guitar. (她会弹吉他)

**表示请求**
- Can I use your phone? (我能用一下你的手机吗？)
- Can you open the window? (你能打开窗户吗？)

**表示允许**
- You can go now. (你现在可以走了)
- I can wait here. (我可以在这儿等)

### 2. 祈使句的用法

**表示命令**
- Stop! (停下！)
- Sit down! (坐下！)

**表示请求**
- Please help me. (请帮帮我)
- Please wait here. (请在这儿等)

**表示建议**
- Take this medicine. (吃这个药)
- Call me tonight. (今晚给我打电话)

### 3. want和need的区别

**want = 想要（主观愿望）**
- I want a new car. (我想要一辆新车)
- She wants to go home. (她想回家)

**need = 需要（客观需求）**
- I need water. (我需要水)
- He needs to see a doctor. (他需要看医生)

## 常见错误

### 1. can的否定形式
- **错误**：I don't can swim.
- **正确**：I cannot (can't) swim.
- **原因**：can的否定直接在can后加not

### 2. 祈使句忘记动词
- **错误**：Not sit here.
- **正确**：Don't sit here.
- **原因**：祈使句需要动词开头

### 3. Where's the... 问法
- **错误**：Where the bathroom is?
- **正确**：Where is the bathroom?
- **原因**：Where引导的特殊疑问句不用助动词

### 4. want to vs want
- **错误**：I want go home.
- **正确**：I want to go home.
- **原因**：want后接动词要用to do形式

### 5. Can I ... 的回答
- **错误**：Yes, you can. / No, you can't.
- **正确**：Yes, you can. / No, you can't. (√)
- **原因**：can问句的回答直接用can

## 学习建议

1. **熟练掌握can的各种句型**：肯定、否定、疑问都要会
2. **牢记问路句型**：Where is...? / How do I get to...? 是最常用的
3. **区分want和need**：want是想要，need是需要
4. **多练习祈使句**：在紧急情况下祈使句非常有用
5. **注意can的弱读**：can在口语中常弱读为/kən/
6. **在真实场景中练习**：迷路了就去问路，主动表达需求
