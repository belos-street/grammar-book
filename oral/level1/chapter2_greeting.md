# Level 1 Chapter 2: 问候、介绍、告别

## 章节目标

- **核心目标**：完成初次见面完整对话
- **语法**：be动词一般现在时 (am / is / are)
- **句型**：Hello/Good morning, How are you? I'm fine, Nice to meet you
- **场景**：打招呼 → 问候 → 自我介绍 → 告别

---

## 知识点概览

### 一、be动词一般现在时

| 人称 | 主语 | be动词 | 缩写 | 否定缩写 |
|------|------|--------|------|----------|
| 第一人称 | I | am | I'm | I'm not |
| 第二人称 | you | are | you're | you aren't |
| 第三人称 | he | is | he's | he isn't |
| 第三人称 | she | is | she's | she isn't |
| 第三人称 | it | is | it's | it isn't |
| 复数 | we/you/they | are | we're/you're/they're | we/you/they aren't |

### 二、常见问候语

**正式场合**
- Good morning. (早上好)
- Good afternoon. (下午好)
- Good evening. (晚上好)

**非正式场合**
- Hello. / Hi. (你好)
- Hey. (嘿)

**回应问候**
- How are you? (你好吗？)
- How are you doing? (你最近怎么样？)
- How's it going? (一切顺利吗？)

### 三、常见回答

**积极回答**
- I'm fine. / I'm good. (我很好)
- I'm great. (我很棒)
- I'm okay. / I'm alright. (我还好)
- Very well, thank you. (非常好，谢谢)

**一般回答**
- Not bad. (还不错)
- So-so. (一般)
- Just so-so. (马马虎虎)

**消极回答**
- I'm not so good. (我不太好)
- I'm tired. (我累了)

### 四、告别用语

**正式场合**
- Goodbye. / Bye. (再见)
- Good night. (晚安)
- See you later. (回头见)
- It was nice meeting you. (很高兴认识你)

**非正式场合**
- Bye-bye. (再见)
- Take care. (保重)
- Catch you later. (回头聊)
- Talk to you later. (回头聊)

## 练习任务

### 任务一：核心句型跟读

**1. 问候语**
- Hello! / Hi!
- Good morning!
- Good afternoon!
- Good evening!

**2. 问候与回答**
- How are you?
- I'm fine, thank you. And you?
- I'm fine too.

**3. 介绍与回应**
- Nice to meet you.
- Nice to meet you too.
- It's nice to meet you.

**4. 告别语**
- Goodbye!
- See you later!
- Take care!

### 任务二：替换练习

**1. 替换姓名和国籍**

原句：Hello, my name is Tom. I'm from America.

- Hello, my name is Mary. I'm from China.
- Hi, my name is Jack. I'm from England.
- Hello, my name is Lucy. I'm from Canada.

**2. 替换状态**

原句：How are you? I'm fine.

- How are you? I'm great.
- How are you? I'm okay.
- How are you? I'm not bad.

**3. 替换告别方式**

原句：Goodbye, see you later!

- Goodbye, take care!
- Bye, talk to you later!
- Goodbye, nice meeting you!

### 任务三：情景对话练习

**情景1：早上遇见朋友**

**A: Good morning, John!**
**B: Good morning, Mary! How are you?**
**A: I'm fine, thank you. How are you?**
**B: I'm fine too. Where are you going?**
**A: I'm going to school. See you later!**
**B: See you!**

**情景2：初次见面介绍**

**A: Hello, my name is Tom. Nice to meet you.**
**B: Hello, Tom. Nice to meet you too. I'm Mary.**
**A: Where are you from, Mary?**
**B: I'm from China. And you?**
**A: I'm from America. Nice meeting you!**
**B: Nice meeting you too!**

### 任务四：纠音练习

**常见发音错误**

1. **good morning**
   - 错误：/gʊd 'mɔːnɪŋ/
   - 正确：/gʊd 'mɔːnɪŋ/
   - 要点：good尾音轻读，morning重音在第一个音节

2. **how are you**
   - 错误：/haʊ ɑː juː/
   - 正确：/haʊ ɑː juː/
   - 要点：how的ow发/aʊ/，are弱读为/ɑː/

3. **nice to meet you**
   - 错误：/naɪs tə miːt jʊ/
   - 正确：/naɪs tə miːt juː/
   - 要点：to弱读为/tə/，you弱读为/jʊː/

4. **goodbye**
   - 错误：/gʊdbaɪ/
   - 正确：/gʊd'baɪ/
   - 要点：goodbye是两个词连读，good的/d/和bye的/b/要连读

## 完整对话练习

### 对话一：正式初次见面

**Setting: 商务会议开场**

**A: Good morning. I'm David Chen. Nice to meet you.**
**B: Good morning, Mr. Chen. Nice to meet you too. I'm Sarah Johnson.**
**A: How are you today, Ms. Johnson?**
**B: I'm very well, thank you. And you?**
**A: I'm fine, thank you. Please, have a seat.**
**B: Thank you.**

> **发音要点**：
> - David /'deɪvɪd/：重音在第一个音节
> - Sarah /'seərə/：重音在第一个音节
> - Johnson /'dʒɒnsən/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话二：非正式朋友介绍

**Setting: 派对上认识新朋友**

**A: Hey, I'm Tom! What's your name?**
**B: Hi Tom, I'm Mary. Nice to meet you!**
**A: Nice to meet you too, Mary! Where are you from?**
**B: I'm from Beijing. You?**
**A: I'm from Shanghai. Oh, are you a student?**
**B: Yes, I am! I'm a university student. You?**
**A: Me too! This is great!**
**B: Yeah! Hey, let's exchange numbers!**
**A: Sure! Here you go!**
**B: Thanks! Talk to you later!**
**A: Bye!**

> **发音要点**：
> - student /'stjuːdənt/：重音在第一个音节
> - Beijing /beɪˈdʒɪŋ/：重音在第二个音节
> - exchange /ɪks'tʃeɪndʒ/：重音在第二个音节

### 对话三：告别场景

**A: Hi Mary, it's been great talking to you!**
**B: Me too, Tom! It was really nice meeting you!**
**A: Yeah! Hey, I have to go now. Take care!**
**B: You too! Goodbye!**
**A: See you later!**
**B: See you!**

## 语法重点

### 1. be动词的用法

**be动词 = 是**

- I am a student. (I am = I'm)
- You are my friend. (You are = You're)
- He is tall. (He is = He's)
- She is nice. (She is = She's)
- It is big. (It is = It's)
- We are here. (We are = We're)
- They are kind. (They are = They're)

### 2. be动词的否定

**not放在be动词后**

- I am not tired. (I'm not)
- You are not wrong. (You aren't)
- He is not here. (He isn't)
- She is not busy. (She isn't)

### 3. be动词的一般疑问句

**be动词提前**

- Are you a student? Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
- Is he home? Yes, he is. / No, he isn't.
- Is she happy? Yes, she is. / No, she isn't.

---

## 常见错误

### 1. 混淆 am / is / are
- **错误**：I is a student. / He are tall.
- **正确**：I am a student. / He is tall.
- **原因**：每个be动词对应特定人称

### 2. 忘记be动词
- **错误**：I a student. / She tall.
- **正确**：I am a student. / She is tall.
- **原因**：英语句子必须有谓语动词

### 3. How are you? 回答用be动词
- **错误**：How are you? I fine.
- **正确**：How are you? I am fine.
- **原因**：回答也要用完整谓语

### 4. Nice to meet you. 误用
- **错误**：Nice to meet you. / Nice to meet you too.
- **正确**：Nice to meet you. / Nice to meet you too.
- **原因**：第一次见面用nice to meet you，分别时用nice meeting you

### 5. Goodbye 拼写或发音错误
- **错误**：Good by.
- **正确**：Goodbye
- **原因**： Goodbye是固定拼写

## 学习建议

1. **背诵核心句型**：把Hello, How are you, Nice to meet you等句子背熟，形成本能反应
2. **注意be动词搭配**：I am, you are, he/she/it is，不能混淆
3. **练习缩写发音**：I'm, you're, he's等缩写在口语中更自然
4. **多听多模仿**：看美剧或听音频，模仿native speaker的语气和节奏
5. **大胆开口**：不要怕犯错，只有多说才能进步
6. **注意场合**：正式场合用Good morning/afternoon/evening，非正式用Hello/Hi
