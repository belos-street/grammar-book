# Level 2 Chapter 6: 天气、穿着、感受

## 章节目标

- **核心目标**：描述天气、穿搭
- **语法**：How's the weather? / 现在进行时（表天气）
- **句型**：It's sunny/rainy/cold. / It's going to rain.
- **场景**：出门穿搭、旅行准备、日常闲聊

---

## 知识点概览

### 一、天气词汇

**天气状况**
- sunny (晴朗的)
- cloudy (多云的)
- rainy (下雨的)
- snowy (下雪的)
- windy (刮风的)
- foggy (有雾的)
- stormy (暴风雨的)

**温度描述**
- hot (热的)
- warm (温暖的)
- cool (凉爽的)
- cold (冷的)
- freezing (极冷的)

**天气变化**
- humid (潮湿的)
- dry (干燥的)
- rainy (下雨的)
- stormy (暴风雨的)

### 二、询问和描述天气

**询问天气**
- What's the weather like? (天气怎么样？)
- How's the weather today? (今天天气怎么样？)
- What's it like outside? (外面天气怎么样？)
- Is it hot outside? (外面热吗？)

**描述天气**
- It's sunny today. (今天阳光明媚)
- It's raining. (正在下雨)
- It's very cold. (很冷)
- It's getting hot. (天气变热了)

**天气+温度**
- It's 25 degrees. (25度)
- It's about 30 degrees Celsius. (大约30摄氏度)
- The temperature is around 20 degrees. (温度大约20度)

### 三、现在进行时

**基本结构**
- 主语 + am/is/are + 动词ing
- I am working. (我正在工作)
- She is reading. (她正在阅读)
- They are playing. (他们正在玩)

**现在进行时表天气（特殊情况）**
- The sun is shining. (阳光照耀)
- It's snowing heavily. (正在下大雪)
- The wind is blowing. (正在刮风)
- It's raining cats and dogs. (正在下倾盆大雨)

### 四、穿衣表达

**询问穿着**
- What should I wear today? (今天我应该穿什么？)
- What are you wearing? (你穿什么？)
- Is this appropriate for the weather? (这个适合这天气吗？)

**建议穿着**
- You should wear a jacket. (你应该穿件外套)
- It's cold, so wear warm clothes. (天冷，穿暖和点)
- It's hot today, wear light clothes. (今天热，穿轻便点)
- Don't forget your umbrella. (别忘了带伞)

**衣物词汇**
- jacket (外套)
- coat (大衣)
- sweater (毛衣)
- T-shirt (T恤)
- jeans (牛仔裤)
- shorts (短裤)
- dress (连衣裙)
- skirt (裙子)
- boots (靴子)
- sandals (凉鞋)
- sneakers (运动鞋)
- umbrella (雨伞)
- sunglasses (太阳镜)
- hat (帽子)
- scarf (围巾)
- gloves (手套)

### 五、表达感受

**冷热感受**
- I'm feeling cold. (我感觉冷)
- I'm so hot. (我好热)
- I'm freezing. (我要冻死了)
- I'm sweating. (我在出汗)

**舒适程度**
- It's comfortable. (很舒适)
- It's nice and warm. (温暖宜人)
- It's too humid. (太潮湿了)
- It's very pleasant today. (今天很宜人)

**天气对活动的影响**
- I can't go out because it's raining. (因为下雨我出不了门)
- The weather is perfect for hiking. (天气很适合徒步)
- It's too hot to exercise. (太热了不能运动)
- I love this kind of weather. (我喜欢这种天气)

## 情景对话练习

### 对话一：日常聊天气

**A: What's the weather like today?**
**B: It's sunny and warm. Beautiful day!**
**A: That sounds nice! Is it hot?**
**B: Not really. It's about 22 degrees, very comfortable.**
**A: Perfect weather for a walk in the park.**
**B: Absolutely! Let's go outside and enjoy it.**

> **发音要点**：
> - comfortable /ˈkʌmftəbəl/：重音在第一个音节
> - absolutely /ˌæbsəˈluːtli/：重音在第二个音节
> - weather /ˈweðə/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话二：穿衣建议

**A: I'm going to Beijing next week. What's the weather like there?**
**B: It's usually cold in winter. You'll need warm clothes.**
**A: What should I pack?**
**B: Bring a heavy coat, sweater, scarf, and gloves.**
**A: Should I bring an umbrella?**
**B: It's dry in winter, so you probably won't need one.**

> **发音要点**：
> - probably /ˈprɒbəbli/：重音在第一个音节
> - winter /ˈwɪntə/：重音在第一个音节
> - heavy /ˈhevi/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话三：天气变化

**A: Look at those clouds! It's going to rain.**
**B: Really? Should we bring an umbrella?**
**A: Yes, definitely. The weather forecast says it's going to storm.**
**B: Oh no! I left my umbrella at home.**
**A: Don't worry. I have one in my bag.**
**B: Great! Thanks for being prepared.**

> **发音要点**：
> - clouds /klaʊdz/：单音节
> - forecast /ˈfɔːkɑːst/：重音在第一个音节
> - prepared /prɪˈpeəd/：重音在第二个音节

### 对话四：旅行准备

**A: What are you planning to wear on your trip?**
**B: I'm thinking of bringing light clothes. It's summer there.**
**A: Yes, but bring a jacket too. It gets cool in the evening.**
**B: Good idea! What about shoes?**
**A: Bring comfortable sneakers. You'll be walking a lot.**
**B: Perfect! Anything else?**
**A: Don't forget sunglasses and sunscreen. It's very sunny.**

> **发音要点**：
> - comfortable /ˈkʌmftəbəl/：重音在第一个音节
> - sneakers /ˈsniːkəz/：重音在第一个音节
> - sunscreen /ˈsʌnskriːn/：重音在第二个音节

## 语法重点

### 1. 现在进行时

**构成：am/is/are + 动词ing**
- I am reading. (我正在阅读)
- She is cooking. (她正在做饭)
- They are playing games. (他们正在玩游戏)

**现在进行时表天气**
- The sun is shining. (阳光照耀)
- It is raining. (正在下雨)
- It is snowing. (正在下雪)

**现在进行时表将来（计划）**
- I am going to the beach tomorrow. (我明天去海滩)
- She is meeting her friends later. (她稍后见朋友)

### 2. be going to 表将来

**预测**
- It's going to rain. (要下雨了)
- It's going to be hot tomorrow. (明天会很热)

**计划**
- I'm going to visit Beijing. (我打算去北京)
- We're going to have a picnic. (我们打算野餐)

### 3. 天气相关表达

**It作为主语**
- It's sunny. (天气晴朗)
- It's raining. (正在下雨)
- It's cold. (天气冷)

**温度表达**
- It's 30 degrees. (30度)
- The temperature is 25 degrees. (温度是25度)
- It's above/below zero. (零上/零下)

## 常见错误

### 1. 现在进行时缺少be动词
- **错误**：It raining.
- **正确**：It is raining.
- **原因**：现在进行时必须有be动词

### 2. weather不可数名词错误
- **错误**：The weather is very weathers.
- **正确**：The weather is very nice.
- **原因**：weather是不可数名词

### 3. wear和put on混淆
- **错误**：I'm wearing a jacket. (正在进行)
- **正确**：I put on a jacket. / I'm wearing a jacket. (√)
- **原因**：wear表示穿的状态，put on表示穿的动作

### 4. 温度表达错误
- **错误**：It's 30 degrees hot.
- **正确**：It's 30 degrees. / It's 30 degrees Celsius.
- **原因**：温度数字后不加形容词

### 5. rainy和rainly混淆
- **错误**：It's rainly today.
- **正确**：It's rainy today.
- **原因**：rain的形容词是rainy

1. **熟练掌握天气词汇**：这些是日常聊天的基础

3. **注意现在进行时的结构**：am/is/are + 动词ing
4. **学习不同场合的穿衣表达**：这对出国旅行很有用
5. **多关注天气预报**：用英语看天气预报是很好的练习
6. **学习描述温度**：摄氏度、华氏度的表达都要会
