### 二、like doing 用法

**基本结构**
- like + doing (喜欢做某事)
- I like swimming. (我喜欢游泳)
- She likes reading. (她喜欢阅读)

**类似用法**
- love + doing (热爱做某事)
- enjoy + doing (享受做某事)
- hate + doing (讨厌做某事)
- dislike + doing (不喜欢做某事)

**例句**
- I love playing basketball. (我热爱打篮球)
- She enjoys watching movies. (她享受看电影)
- He hates running. (他讨厌跑步)

### 三、一般现在时第三人称单数

**be动词变化**
- I am → He is / She is
- You are → He is / She is

**行为动词变化规则**

| 规则 | 原形 | 第三人称单数 |
|------|------|------------|
| 一般加s | like | likes |
| 以s, x, ch, sh, o结尾加es | watch | watches |
| 辅音+y去y加ies | study | studies |
| have | have | has |

**例句**
- I like swimming. → She likes swimming.
- I watch TV every day. → He watches TV every day.
- I study English. → She studies English.
- I have a hobby. → He has a hobby.

### 四、表达兴趣爱好的句型

**直接表达**
- I like swimming. (我喜欢游泳)
- I love reading. (我热爱阅读)
- I enjoy cooking. (我享受烹饪)
- My hobby is painting. (我的爱好是绘画)

**询问爱好**
- What's your hobby? (你的爱好是什么？)
- What do you like doing? (你喜欢做什么？)
- Do you have any hobbies? (你有什么爱好吗？)
- What are you interested in? (你对什么感兴趣？)

**回答方式**
- I like... (我喜欢...)
- I'm interested in... (我对...感兴趣)
- I'm a fan of... (我是...的粉丝)
- My favorite... is... (我最喜欢的...是...)

## 情景对话练习

### 对话一：朋友闲聊兴趣爱好

**A: What do you like doing in your free time?**
**B: I like reading books and watching movies.**
**A: That sounds interesting! What kind of books do you like?**
**B: I like novels, especially mystery novels. How about you?**
**A: I enjoy playing basketball and listening to music.**
**B: Cool! Do you play basketball often?**
**A: Yes, I play every weekend with my friends.**
**B: That's great! I should exercise more.**

> **发音要点**：
> - especially /ɪˈspeʃəli/：重音在第二个音节
> - mystery /ˈmɪstri/：重音在第一个音节
> - exercise /ˈeksəsaɪz/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话二：聊运动

**A: Do you like sports?**
**B: Yes, I love sports! I play tennis and go swimming.**
**A: Which one do you like better?**
**B: I like tennis more. I play it three times a week.**
**A: That's impressive! I'm not very good at sports.**
**B: Don't worry! I can teach you tennis if you want.**
**A: Really? That would be great!**
**B: How about this Saturday?**
**A: Sounds good! Thank you!**

> **发音要点**：
> - impressive /ɪmˈpresɪv/：重音在第二个音节
> - Saturday /ˈsætədeɪ/：重音在第一个音节
> - tennis /ˈtenɪs/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话三：聊电影

**A: What kind of movies do you like?**
**B: I like comedies and action movies.**
**A: Me too! Have you seen the new Marvel movie?**
**B: Yes, I watched it last weekend. It was amazing!**
**A: Really? I want to see it! Who's your favorite actor?**
**B: I like Tom Holland. He's a great Spider-Man.**
**A: He's good! My favorite actress is Scarlett Johansson.**
**B: She's awesome! We should watch a movie together sometime.**
**A: That sounds fun!**

> **发音要点**：
> - comedies /ˈkɒmɪdiz/：重音在第一个音节
> - amazing /əˈmeɪzɪŋ/：重音在第二个音节
> - actress /ˈæktrəs/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话四：破冰聊天

**A: Hi, I'm Tom. Nice to meet you.**
**B: Hi Tom, I'm Sarah. Nice to meet you too.**
**A: So, what do you like doing for fun?**
**B: I enjoy painting and traveling. What about you?**
**A: I like photography and hiking.**
**B: That's cool! Where do you usually go hiking?**
**A: I often go to the mountains near my city.**
**B: I love nature too! Maybe we can go hiking together sometime.**
**A: I'd love that! Let's exchange numbers.**

> **发音要点**：
> - painting /ˈpeɪntɪŋ/：重音在第一个音节
> - photography /fəˈtɒɡrəfi/：重音在第二个音节
> - nature /ˈneɪtʃə/：重音在第一个音节

## 语法重点

### 1. like/love/enjoy + doing

**肯定句**
- I like swimming. (我喜欢游泳)
- She loves reading. (她热爱阅读)
- They enjoy cooking. (他们享受烹饪)

**否定句**
- I don't like swimming. (我不喜欢游泳)
- She doesn't love reading. (她不热爱阅读)
- They don't enjoy cooking. (他们不享受烹饪)

**疑问句**
- Do you like swimming? (你喜欢游泳吗？)
- Does she love reading? (她热爱阅读吗？)
- Do they enjoy cooking? (他们享受烹饪吗？)

### 2. 一般现在时第三人称单数

**规则变化**
- He likes music. (他喜欢音乐)
- She watches TV every day. (她每天看电视)
- He studies English. (他学习英语)

**不规则变化**
- He has a car. (他有一辆车)
- She goes to school. (她去上学)
- He does his homework. (他做作业)

### 3. 频率副词

**常用频率副词**
- always (总是) - 100%
- usually (通常) - 80%
- often (经常) - 60%
- sometimes (有时) - 40%
- rarely (很少) - 20%
- never (从不) - 0%

**位置：在行为动词前，be动词后**
- I always play tennis on weekends.
- She is never late.
- He often watches movies.

## 常见错误

### 1. like后接动词原形
- **错误**：I like swim.
- **正确**：I like swimming.
- **原因**：like后接动词要用doing形式

### 2. 第三人称单数忘记加s
- **错误**：He like swimming.
- **正确**：He likes swimming.
- **原因**：第三人称单数动词要变化

### 3. have的第三人称单数
- **错误**：He haves a hobby.
- **正确**：He has a hobby.
- **原因**：have的第三人称单数是has

### 4. play + 乐器加the
- **错误**：I like playing piano.
- **正确**：I like playing the piano.
- **原因**：play+乐器要加the

### 5. play + 球类不加the
- **错误**：I like playing the basketball.
- **正确**：I like playing basketball.
- **原因**：play+球类运动不加the

## 学习建议

1. **熟练掌握like doing结构**：这是表达兴趣爱好的基础
2. **注意第三人称单数变化**：这是英语学习的重要语法点
3. **背诵兴趣爱好词汇**：分类记忆效果更好
4. **多进行对话练习**：和朋友聊兴趣爱好是最自然的练习方式
5. **注意play的用法**：play+球类不加the，play+乐器要加the
6. **学习频率副词**：让表达更丰富
