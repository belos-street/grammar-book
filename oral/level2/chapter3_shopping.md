# Level 2 Chapter 3: 购物、问价、砍价、退换

## 章节目标

- **核心目标**：买东西完整沟通
- **语法**：How much / 比较级
- **句型**：How much is it? / Can you make it cheaper? / I'll take it.
- **场景**：超市、服装店、网购咨询

---

## 知识点概览

### 一、购物场所词汇

**商店类型**
- supermarket (超市)
- shopping mall (购物中心)
- department store (百货商店)
- clothing store (服装店)
- shoe store (鞋店)
- bookstore (书店)
- grocery store (杂货店)
- convenience store (便利店)
- online store (网店)

**商店区域**
- entrance (入口)
- exit (出口)
- checkout / counter (收银台)
- fitting room (试衣间)
- shelf (货架)
- aisle (过道)

### 二、How much 用法

**询问价格**
- How much is this? (这个多少钱？)
- How much are these? (这些多少钱？)
- How much does it cost? (这个多少钱？)
- What's the price? (价格是多少？)

**How much + 不可数名词**
- How much water do you need? (你需要多少水？)
- How much rice do you want? (你想要多少米饭？)

**How many + 可数名词复数**
- How many apples do you want? (你想要多少苹果？)
- How many shirts do you need? (你需要多少件衬衫？)

### 三、比较级

**构成规则**

| 原级 | 比较级 | 规则 |
|------|--------|------|
| cheap | cheaper | 一般加er |
| big | bigger | 双写辅音加er |
| expensive | more expensive | 多音节词加more |
| good | better | 不规则变化 |
| bad | worse | 不规则变化 |

**常见比较级**
- cheap → cheaper (更便宜)
- expensive → more expensive (更贵)
- big → bigger (更大)
- small → smaller (更小)
- good → better (更好)
- bad → worse (更差)
- long → longer (更长)
- short → shorter (更短)

**比较级句型**
- This one is cheaper than that one. (这个比那个便宜)
- This shirt is more expensive than that one. (这件衬衫比那件贵)
- Which one is better? (哪个更好？)

### 四、购物常用句型

**询问价格**
- How much is this? (这个多少钱？)
- What's the price? (价格是多少？)
- Is this on sale? (这个在打折吗？)

**询问尺寸/颜色**
- Do you have this in a larger size? (这个有大一号的吗？)
- Do you have this in blue? (这个有蓝色的吗？)
- What size is this? (这是什么尺寸？)

**试穿**
- Can I try this on? (我可以试穿吗？)
- Where is the fitting room? (试衣间在哪儿？)
- It fits me well. (很合身)
- It's too big / small. (太大了/太小了)

**砍价**
- Can you make it cheaper? (能便宜点吗？)
- Is there a discount? (有折扣吗？)
- That's too expensive. (太贵了)
- Can you give me a better price? (能给个更好的价格吗？)

**决定购买**
- I'll take it. (我要了)
- I'll buy this one. (我买这个)
- I'll think about it. (我再考虑考虑)

**退换货**
- Can I return this? (这个可以退吗？)
- Can I exchange this? (这个可以换吗？)
- I'd like to return this. (我想退这个)
- Do you have the receipt? (你有收据吗？)

## 情景对话练习

### 对话一：服装店购物

**A: Hello! Can I help you?**
**B: Yes, I'm looking for a shirt.**
**A: What size do you need?**
**B: Medium, please.**
**A: How about this blue one? It's very popular.**
**B: It looks nice. Can I try it on?**
**A: Of course! The fitting room is over there.**
**B: Thank you. (After trying) It fits well. How much is it?**
**A: It's $45.**
**B: That's a bit expensive. Can you make it cheaper?**
**A: Well, I can give you 10% off. That would be $40.50.**
**B: That sounds good. I'll take it.**

> **发音要点**：
> - popular /ˈpɒpjələ/：重音在第一个音节
> - expensive /ɪkˈspensɪv/：重音在第二个音节
> - medium /ˈmiːdiəm/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话二：超市购物

**A: Excuse me, how much are these apples?**
**B: They're $2 per pound.**
**A: And how about these oranges?**
**B: The oranges are $3 per pound.**
**A: Which ones are cheaper?**
**B: The apples are cheaper than the oranges.**
**A: I'll take two pounds of apples, please.**
**B: Sure! Anything else?**
**A: No, that's all. Thank you.**
**B: That'll be $4. Here you go.**

> **发音要点**：
> - apples /ˈæpəlz/：重音在第一个音节
> - oranges /ˈɒrɪndʒɪz/：重音在第一个音节
> - cheaper /ˈtʃiːpə/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话三：砍价

**A: How much is this bag?**
**B: It's $80.**
**A: That's too expensive. Can you make it cheaper?**
**B: This is a high-quality bag. It's worth the price.**
**A: But I've seen similar bags for less. Can you give me a discount?**
**B: Okay, I can give you 15% off. That's $68.**
**A: Can you do better than that? How about $60?**
**B: $65 is my final price.**
**A: Deal! I'll take it.**

> **发音要点**：
> - quality /ˈkwɒləti/：重音在第一个音节
> - similar /ˈsɪmɪlə/：重音在第一个音节
> - discount /ˈdɪskaʊnt/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话四：退换货

**A: Hi, I'd like to return this shirt.**
**B: Sure. Is there a problem with it?**
**A: Yes, it's too small. I need a larger size.**
**B: Do you have the receipt?**
**A: Yes, here it is.**
**B: Okay. Would you like to exchange it for a larger size or get a refund?**
**A: I'd like to exchange it for a large size, please.**
**B: Let me check... Yes, we have it in large. Here you go.**
**A: Thank you so much!**
**B: You're welcome! Is there anything else I can help you with?**

> **发音要点**：
> - receipt /rɪˈsiːt/：重音在第二个音节
> - exchange /ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ/：重音在第二个音节
> - refund /ˈriːfʌnd/：重音在第一个音节

## 语法重点

### 1. How much vs How many

**How much + 不可数名词**
- How much water? (多少水？)
- How much money? (多少钱？)
- How much time? (多少时间？)

**How many + 可数名词复数**
- How many apples? (多少苹果？)
- How many people? (多少人？)
- How many shirts? (多少件衬衫？)

### 2. 比较级的用法

**基本结构**
- A is 比较级 than B. (A比B更...)

**修饰比较级**
- much cheaper (便宜得多)
- a little cheaper (便宜一点)
- even cheaper (甚至更便宜)

**比较级的疑问句**
- Which one is cheaper? (哪个更便宜？)
- Which one is better? (哪个更好？)

### 3. 价格表达

**货币单位**
- dollar(s) (美元)
- cent(s) (美分)
- pound(s) (英镑)
- euro(s) (欧元)
- yuan (人民币)

**价格读法**
- $5.50 → five dollars and fifty cents / five fifty
- $10 → ten dollars
- £20 → twenty pounds

## 常见错误

### 1. How much 和 How many 混用
- **错误**：How many water do you need?
- **正确**：How much water do you need?
- **原因**：water是不可数名词，用How much

### 2. 比较级形式错误
- **错误**：This one is more cheaper.
- **正确**：This one is cheaper.
- **原因**：cheap是单音节词，直接加er

### 3. 价格表达错误
- **错误**：It's five dollar.
- **正确**：It's five dollars.
- **原因**：five是复数，dollar要加s

### 4. than 后面的人称代词
- **错误**：He is taller than me. (口语中可接受)
- **正确**：He is taller than I (am). (正式)
- **原因**：口语中常用宾格，正式场合用主格

### 5. 试穿表达错误
- **错误**：Can I try it?
- **正确**：Can I try it on?
- **原因**：try on是固定搭配，表示"试穿"

## 学习建议

1. **熟练掌握How much/How many**：这是购物的基础
2. **背诵常见比较级**：特别是good-better, bad-worse等不规则变化
3. **多练习砍价对话**：这是实际购物中的常用技能
4. **学习退换货表达**：了解消费者权益相关表达
5. **注意货币单位**：不同国家的货币表达
6. **在真实场景中练习**：去商店主动用英语交流
