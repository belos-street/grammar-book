** extended relatives ( extended relatives (extended relatives)**

- nephew (侄子/外甥)

**家庭成员描述**

- step- (继... )
- half- (半个...，同父异母或同母异父)
- adopted (收养的)

### 二、there be 句型

**基本结构**
- There is + 单数名词
- There are + 复数名词

**肯定句**
- There is a book on the table. (桌上有一本书)
- There are three people in my family. (我家有三口人)
- There is a dog in the garden. (花园里有一只狗)

**否定句**
- There is not (isn't) any milk. (没有牛奶了)
- There are not (aren't) any chairs. (没有椅子了)
- There is no coffee. (= There isn't any coffee.)

**疑问句**
- Is there a bathroom? (有洗手间吗？)
- Yes, there is. / No, there isn't.
- Are there any kids? (有孩子吗？)
- Yes, there are. / No, there aren't.

**some 和 any**
- Some用于肯定句：There are some books.
- Any用于否定句和疑问句：There aren't any books. / Are there any books?

### 三、一般现在时

**be动词**
- I am (I'm) / You are (You're)
- He is (He's) / She is (She's) / It is (It's)
- We are (We're) / They are (They're)

**行为动词**
- I work. / She works.
- They play. / He plays.

**第三人称单数变化**
- 一般加s：work → works, play → plays
- 以s, x, ch, sh, o结尾加es：watch → watches, go → goes
- 辅音+y去y加ies：study → studies, cry → cries

### 四、描述家庭

**介绍家人**
- This is my mother. (这是我妈妈)
- These are my grandparents. (这是我的祖父母)
- That is my brother. (那是我哥哥)
- Those are my cousins. (那些是我表兄弟姐妹)

**家庭成员数量**
- I have a big family. (我有一个大家庭)
- There are five people in my family. (我家有五口人)
- I'm an only child. (我是独生子/女)

**关系描述**
- I get along well with my sister. (我和姐姐相处得很好)
- My parents are very supportive. (我的父母很支持我)
- We are a close family. (我们是一个亲密的家庭)

## 情景对话练习

### 对话一：介绍自己的家庭

**A: Do you have a big family?**
**B: Yes, I do. There are six people in my family.**
**A: Wow! Who are they?**
**B: My parents, my two brothers, my sister, and me.**
**A: That's great! Do you get along well with your siblings?**
**B: Yes, we do. We're very close.**

> **发音要点**：
> - siblings /ˈsɪblɪŋz/：重音在第一个音节
> - close /kləʊs/：形容词时发音
> - parents /ˈpeərənts/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话二：询问对方家庭

**A: Tell me about your family.**
**B: Sure! I have a small family. There are just three of us: my mom, my dad, and me.**
**A: Do you have any pets?**
**B: Yes, we have a dog. His name is Max.**
**A: That's nice! Is your dog friendly?**
**B: Yes, he is very friendly.**

> **发音要点**：
> - friendly /ˈfrendli/：重音在第一个音节
> - pets /pets/：/e/发音
> - just /dʒʌst/：/ʌ/发音

### 对话三：谈论祖父母

**A: Do you see your grandparents often?**
**B: Yes, I do. They live nearby, so I visit them every weekend.**
**A: That's wonderful! What do you do when you visit them?**
**B: We usually have dinner together and my grandma tells me stories.**
**A: That sounds lovely! Are you close to your grandparents?**
**B: Yes, very close. They're like my best friends.**

> **发音要点**：
> - nearby /ˌnɪəˈbaɪ/：重音在第二个音节
> - weekend /ˌwiːkˈend/：重音在第二个音节
> - lovely /ˈlʌvli/：重音在第一个音节

## 语法重点

### 1. there be vs have/has

**there be = 有（表示存在）**
- There is a book on the table. (桌上有书)
- There are three cats in the room. (房间里有三只猫)

**have/has = 有（表示拥有）**
- I have a book. (我有一本书)
- She has three cats. (她有三只猫)

**区别**
- There is a man in the room. (有个人在房间里 - 存在)
- The man has a dog. (这个人有一只狗 - 拥有)

### 2. some 和 any

**some (一些) - 肯定句**
- There are some books on the desk.
- I have some friends.

**any (一些) - 否定句和疑问句**
- There aren't any books on the desk.
- Do you have any questions?

**特殊：some用于问句（表示邀请或希望得到肯定回答）**
- Would you like some coffee? (您想喝咖啡吗？)
- Can I have some help? (我能得到一些帮助吗？)

### 3. 可数名词和不可数名词

**可数名词**
- 可以用数字计数：one book, two books
- 有复数形式：book → books
- 可以和a/an连用：a book

**不可数名词**
- 不能用数字直接计数：water, milk, rice
- 没有复数形式：water (不用waters)
- 不能和a/an直接连用：a glass of water

**常见不可数名词**
- water, milk, coffee, tea, juice
- rice, bread, butter, cheese
- money, time, information
- music, homework, advice

## 常见错误

### 1. there be 和 have/has 混用
- **错误**：There has a book. / I have a book in the table.
- **正确**：There is a book. / There is a book on the table.
- **原因**：there be表示存在，have表示拥有

### 2. some和any混用
- **错误**：There are some people outside? (疑问句)
- **正确**：Are there any people outside?
- **原因**：疑问句和否定句用any

### 3. 可数名词单独使用
- **错误**：I need a advice. / Give me a information.
- **正确**：I need some advice. / Give me some information.
- **原因**：advice和information是不可数名词

### 4. family作为主语的动词
- **错误**：My family is all teachers.
- **正确**：My family are all teachers. (英式英语)
- **原因**：family在美式英语中用单数，在英式英语中可用复数

### 5. there is/are 的就近原则
- **错误**：There is a table and six chairs.
- **正确**：There is a table and six chairs. (√)
- **原因**：离be动词最近的名词决定用is还是are
