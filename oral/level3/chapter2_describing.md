## 知识点概览

### 一、外貌描述词汇

**身高**
- tall (高的)
- short (矮的)
- medium height (中等身高)
- tall and slim (高挑)
- short and stocky (矮壮)

**体型**
- thin (瘦的)
- slim (苗条的)
- average (普通的)
- overweight (超重的)
- muscular (肌肉发达的)
- chubby (圆润的)

**头发**
- long (长的)
- short (短的)
- curly (卷曲的)
- straight (直的)
- wavy (波浪的)
- bald (秃头的)
- black hair (黑发)
- brown hair (棕发)
- blonde hair (金发)
- red hair (红发)

**眼睛**
- big eyes (大眼睛)
- small eyes (小眼睛)
- brown eyes (棕色眼睛)
- blue eyes (蓝色眼睛)
- green eyes (绿色眼睛)

**其他特征**
- beard (胡须)
- mustache (八字胡)
- glasses (眼镜)
- freckles (雀斑)

### 二、性格描述词汇

**正面性格**
- friendly (友好的)
- kind (善良的)
- honest (诚实的)
- humorous (幽默的)
- confident (自信的)
- reliable (可靠的)
- generous (慷慨的)
- patient (耐心的)
- smart / intelligent (聪明的)
- talented (有才华的)

**负面性格**
- shy (害羞的)
- lazy (懒惰的)
- selfish (自私的)
- rude (粗鲁的)
- stubborn (固执的)
- impatient (不耐烦的)
- dishonest (不诚实的)

**中性描述**
- quiet (安静的)
- talkative (健谈的)
- serious (严肃的)
- cheerful (开朗的)
- sensitive (敏感的)

### 三、形容词排序

**基本规则（OSASCOMP）**

| 顺序 | 类别 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| O - Opinion | 观点 | beautiful, nice |
| S - Size | 大小 | tall, small |
| A - Age | 年龄 | young, old |
| S - Shape | 形状 | thin, fat |
| C - Color | 颜色 | red, blue |
| O - Origin | 来源 | Chinese, American |
| M - Material | 材料 | wooden, plastic |
| P - Purpose | 用途 | sleeping (bag) |

**例句**
- He is a tall young Chinese man. (他是一个高个子的年轻中国男人)
- She has long black straight hair. (她有长的黑色直发)
- I bought a beautiful small red leather bag. (我买了一个漂亮的红色小皮包)
- This is an old wooden dining table. (这是一张旧的木制餐桌)

### 四、定语从句基础

**基本结构**
- 主句 + who/which/that + 描述

**who (指人)**
- The man who is wearing a blue shirt is my teacher. (穿蓝色衬衫的那个人是我老师)
- She is the girl who sits next to me. (她就是坐我旁边的那个女孩)

**which/that (指物)**
- The book which/that is on the table is mine. (桌上的那本书是我的)
- The car which/that is parked outside is John's. (停在外面那辆车是约翰的)

**先行词 + that (万能)**
- The person that helped me was very kind. (帮助我的那个人很善良)
- The movie that we watched was interesting. (我们看的那部电影很有趣)

## 练习任务

### 任务一：描述外貌练习

**1. 描述一个人**
- He is tall with short black hair.
- She has big blue eyes and a friendly smile.
- He wears glasses and has a beard.

**2. 用形容词描述**
- My friend is tall, handsome, and friendly.
- The teacher is middle-aged with gray hair.

### 任务二：定语从句练习

**1. 用who连接**
- The man → He is my brother → The man who is my brother...
- The girl → She sits in front → The girl who sits in front...

**2. 用which/that连接**
- The book → It's on the table → The book which is on the table...
- The house → It's beautiful → The house that is beautiful...

### 任务三：综合描述练习

**描述朋友**
- My best friend is a tall, friendly girl who has long brown hair.
- She is always cheerful and helpful.

**描述物品**
- I have a small red car that was made in Japan.
- It's a comfortable leather chair which costs a lot.

## 情景对话练习

### 对话一：描述失踪的朋友

**A: Have you seen my friend? He's tall with black hair.**
**B: What does he look like?**
**A: He's about 180cm tall, medium build, and wears glasses.**
**B: Is he the one who was wearing a blue jacket?**
**A: Yes, that's him! Did you see where he went?**
**B: He went towards the exit a few minutes ago.**
**A: Thank you so much!**

> **发音要点**：
> - medium /ˈmiːdiəm/：重音在第一个音节
> - build /bɪld/：单音节
> - exit /ˈeksɪt/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话二：介绍新朋友

**A: I want you to meet my friend Sarah.**
**B: Nice to meet you, Sarah! What does she look like?**
**A: She has long blonde hair and blue eyes.**
**B: What about her personality?**
**A: She's really friendly and humorous. You'll like her.**
**B: That sounds great! How did you meet her?**
**A: We met at university. She's the girl who always makes everyone laugh.**

> **发音要点**：
> - personality /ˌpɜːsəˈnæləti/：重音在第三个音节
> - humorous /ˈhjuːmərəs/：重音在第一个音节
> - university /ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəti/：重音在第三个音节

### 对话三：描述物品

**A: Your apartment is nice! Is that a new sofa?**
**B: Yes, it's a leather sofa which I bought last month.**
**A: It looks comfortable. What color is it?**
**B: It's dark brown. I have a coffee table that matches it too.**
**A: Great! Do you also have a TV that is big?**
**B: Yes, I have a 65-inch TV. Let me show you around!**

> **发音要点**：
> - apartment /əˈpɑːtmənt/：重音在第二个音节
> - leather /ˈleðə/：重音在第一个音节
> - comfortable /ˈkʌmftəbəl/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话四：描述理想伴侣

**A: What kind of person are you looking for?**
**B: I want someone who is kind and honest.**
**A: What about appearance?**
**B: I prefer someone who is tall with a nice smile.**
**A: Do you have any other requirements?**
**B: I'd like someone who has a good sense of humor and enjoys traveling.**
**A: That's specific! Good luck with your search!**

> **发音要点**：
> - appearance /əˈpɪərəns/：重音在第二个音节
> - requirements /rɪˈkwaɪəmənts/：重音在第二个音节
> - humor /ˈhjuːmə/：重音在第一个音节

## 语法重点

### 1. 形容词排序记忆口诀

**OSASCOMP**
- Opinion → Size → Age → Shape → Color → Origin → Material → Purpose

**例句演示**
- She's a beautiful young girl. (观点-年龄)
- He has long black hair. (长度-颜色)
- I bought an expensive Italian leather jacket. (价格-来源-材料)

### 2. 定语从句的关系词

**who - 指人**
- The teacher who teaches English is Mr. Wang.
- The students who arrived early got good seats.

**which/that - 指物**
- The book which/that I bought is interesting.
- The phone which/that is on the desk is mine.

**that - 万能（指人或物）**
- The person that helped me was kind.
- The movie that we saw was great.

### 3. 描述性形容词 vs 限定性形容词

**描述性（可有可无）**
- My brother, who lives in Beijing, is a doctor. (补充说明)

**限定性（必不可少）**
- The man who is wearing a hat is my father. (限定是谁)

## 常见错误

### 1. 形容词排序错误
- **错误**：a black tall hat
- **正确**：a tall black hat
- **原因**：大小在前，颜色在后

### 2. 定语从句缺少关系词
- **错误**：The man is my teacher works here.
- **正确**：The man who works here is my teacher.
- **原因**：定语从句必须有who/which/that连接

### 3. who和which混用
- **错误**：The girl which is tall is my sister.
- **正确**：The girl who is tall is my sister.
- **原因**：who指人，which指物

### 4. 定语从句中主谓不一致
- **错误**：The boy who is playing tennis plays well.
- **正确**：The boy who is playing tennis plays well. (√)
- **原因**：定语从句的动词要和先行词一致

### 5. 描述外貌时缺少be动词
- **错误**：He tall with black hair.
- **正确**：He is tall with black hair.
- **原因**：描述外貌必须用be动词
