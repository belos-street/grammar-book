# Level 3 Chapter 4: 计划、周末、未来目标

## 章节目标

- **核心目标**：说未来安排
- **语法**：一般将来时 will / be going to
- **句型**：I'm going to… / I plan to…
- **场景**：周末计划、假期旅行、未来打算

---

## 知识点概览

### 一、一般将来时

**will + 动词原形**

| 句型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定句 | 主语 + will + 动词 | I will call you later. |
| 否定句 | 主语 + won't + 动词 | I won't go there. |
| 疑问句 | Will + 主语 + 动词？ | Will you come? |

**be going to + 动词原形**

| 句型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定句 | 主语 + be going to + 动词 | I'm going to study. |
| 否定句 | 主语 + be not going to + 动词 | I'm not going to go. |
| 疑问句 | Be + 主语 + going to + 动词？ | Are you going to come? |

### 二、will vs be going to

**will - 临时决定/预测**
- I think it will rain tomorrow. (预测)
- I'll help you with that. (临时决定)
- Wait, I'll open the door. (临时决定)

**be going to - 计划/准备**
- I'm going to travel next month. (计划)
- We're going to move to a new house. (计划)
- I'm going to learn Spanish. (打算)

### 三、计划与意图表达

**表达计划**
- I plan to... (我计划...)
- I'm planning to... (我正在计划...)
- I'm going to... (我打算...)
- I've decided to... (我决定...)
- I intend to... (我意图...)

**表达愿望**
- I want to... (我想...)
- I'd like to... (我想要...)
- I hope to... (我希望...)
- I wish to... (我希望...)

**询问计划**
- What are you going to do this weekend? (这周末你打算做什么？)
- Do you have any plans for the summer? (夏天你有什么计划吗？)
- Are you going to... ? (你打算...吗？)

### 四、周末活动词汇

**放松活动**
- watch a movie (看电影)
- hang out with friends (和朋友出去)
- go shopping (购物)
- have a picnic (野餐)
- read a book (看书)
- sleep in (睡懒觉)

**学习活动**
- study for exams (准备考试)
- take a course (上课)
- practice English (练习英语)
- do homework (做作业)

**运动活动**
- go to the gym (去健身房)
- play sports (做运动)
- go hiking (徒步)
- ride a bike (骑自行车)

## 情景对话练习

### 对话一：周末计划

**A: Hey, do you have any plans for this weekend?**
**B: Yeah, I'm going to go hiking with some friends. Do you want to come?**
**A: That sounds great! When are you planning to leave?**
**B: We're going to meet at 7 a.m. at the bus station.**
**A: Ok, I'll pick you up at 6:30.**
**B: Perfect! See you Saturday morning.**

### 对话三：未来目标

**A: Where do you see yourself in 5 years?**
**B: I'm going to start my own business.**
**A: That's ambitious! What kind of business?**
**B: I'm planning to open a restaurant.**

**B: First, I'm going to work in a restaurant to gain experience.**

### 对话四：临时改变计划

**A: Hey, are you still going to the concert tonight?**
**B: Actually, I'm not going to make it. Something came up.**

**B: My friend just called. She needs help with an emergency.**

## 语法重点

### 1. will 的用法

**表预测**
- I think it will be hot tomorrow.
- She will probably succeed.

**表临时决定**
- Wait, I'll help you.
- I'll open the window.

**表承诺**
- I will always love you.
- I will never forget this.

### 2. be going to 的用法

**表计划**
- I'm going to visit my parents next week.
- They're going to buy a new house.

**表打算**
- I'm going to learn driving this summer.
- She's going to apply for that job.

### 3. 常见时间状语

**表未来时间**
- tomorrow (明天)
- next week/month/year (下周/月/年)
- soon (很快)
- in the future (将来)
- one day (有一天)

---

## 常见错误

### 1. will和be going to混用
- **错误**：I will go to the store tomorrow.
- **正确**：I'm going to go to the store tomorrow. (计划) / I will go. (决定)
- **原因**：will表临时决定，be going to表计划

### 2. be going to后接动词原形
- **错误**：I'm going to went shopping.
- **正确**：I'm going to go shopping.
- **原因**：be going to后必须接动词原形

### 3. will后接动词ing
- **错误**：I will going to help you.
- **正确**：I will help you.
- **原因**：will后接动词原形

### 4. 第三人称单数
- **错误**：He going to travel.
- **正确**：He's going to travel.
- **原因**：主语后要有be动词

### 5. 否定形式
- **错误**：I won't to go there.
- **正确**：I'm not going to go there. / I won't go there.
- **原因**：won't后接动词原形，be going to否定直接在be后加not

## 学习建议

1. **区分will和be going to**：理解两者的使用场景
2. **多表达自己的计划**：用英语描述自己的周末和假期
3. **学习未来时间状语**：tomorrow, next week等
4. **用I plan to/I intend to表达**：丰富表达方式
5. **练习对话**：询问和回答关于计划的问题
6. **制定自己的计划**：用英语列出自己的目标
