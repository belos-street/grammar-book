# Level 3 Chapter 3: 回忆过去、旅行、经历

## 章节目标

- **核心目标**：讲过去发生的事
- **语法**：一般过去时 / 过去进行时
- **句型**：I went to… / We were…
- **场景**：旅行经历、童年、难忘的事

---

## 知识点概览

### 一、一般过去时

**基本结构**
- 肯定句：主语 + 动词过去式
- 否定句：主语 + did not (didn't) + 动词原形
- 疑问句：Did + 主语 + 动词原形？

**规则动词过去式**
- 一般加-ed：play → played, work → worked
- 以e结尾加-d：like → liked, love → loved
- 辅音+y去y加-ied：study → studied, try → tried
- 双写辅音加-ed：stop → stopped, plan → planned

**不规则动词（常见）**
- go → went
- have → had
- eat → ate
- see → saw
- take → took
- come → came
- make → made
- get → got
- know → knew
- think → thought
- say → said
- tell → told
- find → found
- give → gave
- leave → left
- feel → felt
- put → put
- read → read
- become → became

**时间状语**
- yesterday (昨天)
- last week/month/year (上周/月/年)
- ago (以前)
- in 2020 (在2020年)
- the day before yesterday (前天)
- this morning (今天早上)

### 二、过去进行时

**基本结构**
- 肯定句：主语 + was/were + 动词ing
- 否定句：主语 + was/were + not + 动词ing
- 疑问句：Was/Were + 主语 + 动词ing？

**用法**
- 表示过去正在进行的动作
- 表示在过去某一时刻正在发生的事
- 与 when/while/as 连用

**例句**
- I was reading a book when you called. (你打电话时我正在看书)
- While she was cooking, the phone rang. (她做饭时电话响了)
- We were sleeping at midnight. (午夜时我们正在睡觉)

**was/were 的选择**
- I/he/she/it → was
- you/we/they → were

### 三、旅行相关词汇

**旅行活动**
- go sightseeing (观光)
- take photos (拍照)
- try local food (尝试当地美食)
- visit museums (参观博物馆)
- go to the beach (去海滩)
- hike in the mountains (在山上徒步)

**旅行感受**
- amazing (令人惊叹的)
- breathtaking (令人屏息的)
- unforgettable (难忘的)
- exhausting (令人疲惫的)
- relaxing (放松的)
- incredible (难以置信的)

**旅行问题**
- get lost (迷路)
- miss the flight (错过航班)
- catch a cold (感冒)
- lose my wallet (丢钱包)

### 四、描述经历的句型

**旅行经历**
- I went to Paris last summer. (去年夏天我去了巴黎)
- We visited the Eiffel Tower. (我们参观了埃菲尔铁塔)
- The food was delicious. (食物很美味)
- It was an amazing experience. (这是一次很棒的经历)

**童年回忆**
- When I was young, I used to play in the park. (我小时候常在公园玩)
- I remember my grandmother's cooking. (我记得奶奶做的饭)
- Those were the happy days. (那是快乐的日子)

**难忘的事**
- The most unforgettable moment was... (最难忘的时刻是...)
- I'll never forget the day when... (我永远不会忘记...那天)
- That experience changed my life. (那次经历改变了我的生活)

**1. 动词过去式变化**
- play → played
- go → went
- eat → ate

**2. 肯定句改否定句**
- I went to school. → I didn't go to school.
- She ate breakfast. → She didn't eat breakfast.

**3. 肯定句改疑问句**
- He played football. → Did he play football?
- They came here. → Did they come here?

### 任务二：过去进行时练习

**1. be动词选择**
- I → was
- They → were
- She → was
- We → were

**2. 用过去进行时造句**
- I was reading when she came.
- They were playing while it was raining.

## 情景对话练习

### 对话一：聊假期旅行

**A: Did you go anywhere during the holidays?**
**B: Yes, I went to Italy for two weeks.**
**A: That sounds amazing! Which cities did you visit?**
**B: I visited Rome, Florence, and Venice.**
**A: Wow! What was your favorite part?**
**B: I think the Colosseum in Rome was incredible.**
**A: Did you try any Italian food?**
**B: Of course! The pasta was absolutely delicious.**

> **发音要点**：
> - Colosseum /ˌkɒləˈsiːəm/：重音在第三个音节
> - incredible /ɪnˈkredəbəl/：重音在第二个音节
> - absolutely /ˌæbsəˈluːtli/：重音在第二个音节

### 对话二：回忆童年

**A: Tell me about your childhood. What did you use to do?**
**B: I used to spend summers at my grandparents' house.**
**A: That sounds wonderful! What did you do there?**
**B: I used to play in the garden and help my grandmother cook.**
**A: Do you still visit them?**
**B: Yes, but not as often. They moved to the countryside last year.**

> **发音要点**：
> - childhood /ˈtʃaɪldhʊd/：重音在第一个音节
> - grandparents /ˈɡrænpeərənts/：重音在第一个音节
> - countryside /ˈkʌntrisaɪd/：重音在第二个音节

### 对话三：难忘的经历

**A: What's the most unforgettable experience you've ever had?**
**B: That would be my trip to Machu Picchu in Peru.**
**A: That sounds amazing! What happened?**
**B: We were hiking when the sun came out and lit up the ruins.**
**A: That must have been breathtaking!**
**B: It was! I took hundreds of photos. I'll never forget that moment.**

> **发音要点**：
> - Machu Picchu /ˌmætʃuː ˈpiːktʃuː/：重音在第一个音节
> - ruins /ˈruːɪnz/：重音在第一个音节
> - breathtaking /ˈbreθteɪkɪŋ/：重音在第二个音节

### 对话四：遗憾的事

**A: Do you have any regrets in life?**
**B: I regret not studying harder when I was young.**
**A: Why do you feel that way?**
**B: If I had studied harder, I might have gotten into a better university.**
**A: But you're doing well now, aren't you?**
**B: Yes, I am. But sometimes I wonder what could have been.**

> **发音要点**：
> - regrets /rɪˈɡrets/：重音在第二个音节
> - university /ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəti/：重音在第三个音节
> - wondering /ˈwʌndərɪŋ/：重音在第一个音节

## 语法重点

### 1. 一般过去时 vs 过去进行时

**一般过去时 - 强调动作已完成**
- I read a book yesterday. (我昨天读了一本书 - 已读完)
- She called me last night. (她昨晚给我打了电话 - 已打完)

**过去进行时 - 强调动作在进行中**
- I was reading a book at 8 o'clock. (8点时我正在读书 - 在读的过程中)
- She was calling someone when I came. (我来时她正在打电话)

### 2. when vs while

**when + 从句（动作先发生）**
- When I arrived, she was leaving. (我到时她正要离开)

**while + 从句（动作同时发生）**
- While I was waiting, it started to rain. (我等待时开始下雨)

### 3. used to 的用法

**过去习惯（现在不再）**
- I used to play basketball. (我以前常打篮球 - 现在不打了)
- She used to live in Beijing. (她以前住在北京 - 现在不住了)

**与 would 的区别**
- used to 更强调"过去是这样，现在不是了"
- would 更常用于故事叙述

## 常见错误

### 1. 规则动词忘记加-ed
- **错误**：I play football yesterday.
- **正确**：I played football yesterday.
- **原因**：一般过去时要加-ed

### 2. 不规则动词使用错误
- **错误**：I goed to the store.
- **正确**：I went to the store.
- **原因**：go的过去式是went，不是goed

### 3. 过去进行时缺少be动词
- **错误**：I reading a book.
- **正确**：I was reading a book.
- **原因**：过去进行时必须有was/were

### 4. when和while后动词形式错误
- **错误**：When I was arriving, she left.
- **正确**：When I arrived, she left. / When I was arriving, she was leaving.
- **原因**：when后接短暂动作，while后接进行动作

### 5. used to和use to混淆
- **错误**：I use to go to school by bus.
- **正确**：I used to go to school by bus.
- **原因**：used to是固定搭配
