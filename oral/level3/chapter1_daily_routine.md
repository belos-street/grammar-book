# Level 3 Chapter 1: 日常作息、工作、学习

## 章节目标

- **核心目标**：描述一天安排
- **语法**：频率副词(always/usually) / 一般现在时
- **句型**：I get up at… / I usually…
- **场景**：聊工作、聊学习、日常安排

---

## 知识点概览

### 一、日常作息词汇

**早晨活动**
- get up (起床)
- brush teeth (刷牙)
- take a shower (淋浴)
- have breakfast (吃早餐)
- get dressed (穿衣服)
- check phone (看手机)

**上午活动**
- go to work/school (上班/上学)
- have a meeting (开会)
- have classes (上课)
- do homework (做作业)
- have lunch (吃午餐)

**下午活动**
- continue working (继续工作)
- have afternoon classes (下午课)
- take a break (休息)
- do exercise (锻炼)
- have dinner (吃晚餐)

**晚间活动**
- watch TV (看电视)
- read a book (看书)
- study English (学英语)
- spend time with family (陪家人)
- go to bed (睡觉)

### 二、频率副词

**频率等级（从高到低）**

| 频率副词 | 频率 | 中文 |
|---------|------|------|
| always | 100% | 总是 |
| usually | 80% | 通常 |
| often | 60% | 经常 |
| sometimes | 40% | 有时 |
| rarely | 20% | 很少 |
| never | 0% | 从不 |

**位置：行为动词前，be动词后**
- I always get up early. (我总是早起)
- She is usually late. (她通常迟到)
- They often eat out. (他们经常外出吃饭)

### 三、一般现在时

**基本用法**
- 表示习惯性动作
- 表示客观事实
- 表示普遍真理

**时间状语**
- every day (每天)
- every morning (每天早上)
- every week (每周)
- on weekdays (在工作日)
- on weekends (在周末)

**第三人称单数变化**
- work → works
- study → studies
- go → goes
- have → has

### 四、描述日常安排

**描述一天行程**
- I get up at 6:30 every morning. (我每天早上6:30起床)
- I usually have breakfast at 7:00. (我通常7点吃早餐)
- I leave home at 8:00. (我8点出门)

**描述工作/学习**
- I work at a company. (我在一家公司工作)
- I'm a software engineer. (我是一名软件工程师)
- I study at Beijing University. (我在北京大学学习)

**询问日常**
- What time do you usually wake up? (你通常几点醒来？)
- What do you do in the morning? (你早上做什么？)
- How do you spend your weekends? (你周末怎么过？)

## 情景对话练习

### 对话一：聊工作日常

**A: What do you do for a living?**
**B: I'm a marketing manager at a tech company.**
**A: That sounds interesting! What's a typical day like for you?**
**B: I usually get up at 6:30 and leave home at 8:00.**
**A: Do you often work overtime?**
**B: Sometimes, especially when we have product launches.**
**A: How do you handle work stress?**
**B: I usually exercise after work. It helps me relax.**

> **发音要点**：
> - marketing /ˈmɑːkɪtɪŋ/：重音在第一个音节
> - overtime /ˈəʊvətaɪm/：重音在第一个音节
> - manage /ˈmænɪdʒ/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话二：聊学习日常

**A: You're a university student, right? What's your major?**
**B: Yes, I'm majoring in Computer Science.**
**A: How's your daily schedule?**
**B: Well, I usually wake up at 7:00 and have classes from 9:00 to 12:00.**
**A: Do you have afternoon classes every day?**
**B: No, I usually have lab work on Tuesday and Thursday afternoons.**
**A: What do you do in your free time?**
**B: I often study at the library or hang out with friends.**

> **发音要点**：
> - university /ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəti/：重音在第三个音节
> - major /ˈmeɪdʒə/：重音在第一个音节
> - schedule /ˈʃedjuːl/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话三：聊周末安排

**A: How do you usually spend your weekends?**
**B: On Saturdays, I often sleep in and do chores in the morning.**
**A: What do you usually do in the afternoon?**
**B: I sometimes go shopping or meet friends for lunch.**
**A: Do you have any hobbies?**
**B: Yes, I love hiking. I usually go to the mountains on Sundays.**
**A: That sounds great! How long have you been hiking?**
**B: I've been hiking for about two years now.**

> **发音要点**：
> - chores /tʃɔːz/：单音节
> - mountains /ˈmaʊntɪnz/：重音在第一个音节
> - hiking /ˈhaɪkɪŋ/：重音在第一个音节

### 对话四：聊时间管理

**A: You always seem so organized. Any tips?**
**B: I always make a to-do list every morning.**
**A: Do you usually stick to your schedule?**
**B: Mostly. But sometimes unexpected things come up.**
**A: How do you balance work and life?**
**B: I try to leave work at the office and not bring it home.**
**A: That's smart. Do you exercise regularly?**
**B: Yes, I go to the gym three times a week.**

> **发音要点**：
> - organized /ˈɔːɡənaɪzd/：重音在第二个音节
> - unexpected /ˌʌnɪkˈspektɪd/：重音在第三个音节
> - regularly /ˈreɡjələli/：重音在第一个音节

## 语法重点

### 1. 频率副词的用法

**位置**
- 在be动词后：She is always happy.
- 在行为动词前：I usually wake up early.

**修饰动词**
- I always forget my keys. (我总是忘记带钥匙)
- He rarely exercises. (他很少锻炼)

**提问**
- How often do you exercise? (你多久锻炼一次？)
- Do you always eat breakfast? (你总是吃早餐吗？)

### 2. 一般现在时的时间状语

**每日**
- every day (每天)
- every single day (每天)

**每周**
- every week (每周)
- on weekdays (在工作日)
- on weekends (在周末)

**其他**
- in the morning (在早上)
- at night (在晚上)
- once a week (每周一次)
- twice a month (每月两次)

### 3. 现在完成时（谈论经验）

**构成：have/has + 过去分词**
- I have finished my homework. (我已完成作业)
- She has worked here for 5 years. (她在这儿工作5年了)

**have been vs have gone**
- I have been to Beijing. (我去过北京 - 去过已回)
- He has gone to Shanghai. (他去上海了 - 去了未回)

## 常见错误

### 1. 频率副词位置错误
- **错误**：I get up always early.
- **正确**：I always get up early.
- **原因**：频率副词放在行为动词前

### 2. 频率副词与be动词位置错误
- **错误**：I am not always late.
- **正确**：I am not always late. (√)
- **原因**：be动词后也放频率副词

### 3. 第三人称单数忘记加s
- **错误**：He work hard every day.
- **正确**：He works hard every day.
- **原因**：第三人称单数动词要变化

### 4. get up + 时间用介词错误
- **错误**：I get up in 6:30.
- **正确**：I get up at 6:30.
- **原因**：具体时间点用at

### 5. everyday vs every day混淆
- **错误**：I study everyday English.
- **正确**：I study English every day.
- **原因**：everyday是形容词（日常的），every day是副词短语

## 学习建议

1. **用英语记录日程**：每天用英语写待办事项
2. **描述自己的日常**：每天用英语描述3件做的事
3. **学习时间表达**：at 6:30, in the morning等
4. **练习频率副词**：用不同频率副词描述习惯
5. **多和他人交流**：分享你的日常，听他人的日常
6. **关注时间状语**：学习更多的时间状语表达
